High-Level Pickleball Clinic: Weekend Workshop

This structured clinic is designed for competitive and advanced players (3.5+ DUPR), focusing on refining techniques, strategic play, and match performance through intensive training.

Workshop Details:

  • **Duration:** Weekend Workshop (6 hours total)
  • **Session Length:** 3 hours per day (e.g., 90 min before lunch, 90 min after)
  • **Participants:** 6-16 players (4 per court)
  • **Skill Level:** Advanced-Intermediate/Advanced/Competitive (3.5+ DUPR)

Key Focus Areas:

  • Advanced Strategy & Patterns (Building Points, Dinks, Drops, Resets, Court Coverage)
  • Movement & Power Control (Speedups, Smashes, Blocks & Counters)
  • Spin Play (Topspin/Slice Defense, Transition Skills)
  • Match Play & Tournament Strategy (Positioning, Mental Game)

Junior Pickleball Camps!

Give your child the gift of pickleball this summer! Our camps offer fun, engaging instruction for all skill levels, focusing on skill development, sportsmanship, and making new friends.

Our dedicated youth coach, Coach Barry, leads our junior programs. He works with talented young players like Aidan, our 10-year-old paddle-sponsored prodigy.

**Aidan's Bio:** Aidan started playing tennis at age 5, then picked up pickleball in June 2020. He became more interested at age 8 and now plays tennis and pickleball equally. In 2024, he played 5 junior PPA events, earning 3 medals in boys doubles and was the only 9-year-old to medal in boys singles. So far in 2025, he’s played 2 junior events, earning gold in boys singles and silver in boys doubles and mixed doubles.
